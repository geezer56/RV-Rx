Our Mission

Too many times the first story I hear from a RVer is a sad tale about the 1st unit they owned. Their pleasant dreams of travelling the country dashed by an unscrupulous dealer or private seller who was only too happy to see their nightmare of a coach or trailer disappear around the corner. Just about the time the seller was cashing the check, the buyer was discovering that the "pristine" RV they just spent their hard earned dollars for was not all that the seller had made it out to be. The dream had turned into a reality of ruined vacations and thousands of dollars wasted.

At RV Rx we are committed to making sure that when you start telling YOUR RV stories, that 1st cautionary tale is not included. By providing a complete set of RV services, including the most comprehensive inspection in the industry, we can provide you with the information and systems know-how that you will need to beginn your RV journey with confidence. Our inspection X-Ray will ensure that you know everything you need to negotiate the best price or even walk away from a potential disaster.

Inspection

All inspections are performed by a NRVIA level 2 certified inspector. An inspection is not just a good idea for the purchaser. Private sellers, insurance companies, banks and warranty companies all have varying degrees of interest in understanding the condition of the unit that is being considered. The inspection results however are the sole property of our client and will not be shared with anyone else without explicit permission of our client.
