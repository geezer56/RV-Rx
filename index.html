Inspection Services

Our Esential Plus inspection provides a complete X-ray of the subject unit. This is valuable whether you are considering a purchase or just want a complete check up on your Old Faithful. Depending on the size, complexity and the inspection site this inspection may take 6 to 8 hours. A full report is generated catergorizing issues into Life Safety, Major and Minor. We will include over 40 photos and descriptions of any issues we encounter. Here is a summary of what we include in our report>
  • Engine compartment and components, cab and dash controls, windshield
  • Roof, roof vents, skylights, A/C, seams and seals, antenna
  • Sidewalls, front and rear caps, cargo bays, slide outs, toppers, windows, entry doors, awnings, seams and seals
  • Chassis, running/marker/clearance lights, wheel/tire assemblies, air bags, hitch, leveling system
  • Electrical systems including panels, batteries, power converter, inverter, generator
  • Water and waste system, water pump, plumbing, monitor panels
  • Refrigerator, water heater, furnace, air conditioner, all other appliances and electronics
  • Propane system, LP/CO smoke detectors
  • Coach interior including all lights, outlets, window operability, furniture, flooring, bathroom
